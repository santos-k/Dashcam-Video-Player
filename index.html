Dashcam Player

A powerful dual-camera dashcam video player for Windows. Play, sync, and export front & back camera footage with ease.

⬇ Download Installer ★ View on GitHub
v1.1.1 · Windows x64

Features

Everything you need to review dashcam footage

📷

Dual Camera Sync

Automatically detects and pairs front & back camera files from your dashcam SD card. Fine-tune audio sync with millisecond offset control.

🎬

Flexible Layouts

Switch between Side-by-side, Stacked, and Picture-in-Picture views. PIP is draggable and resizable with position memory.

🌎

GPS Map Integration

Extracts GPS coordinates from video metadata and displays the location on an interactive OpenStreetMap sidebar with multiple tile layers.

💾

Export & Save

Export composed videos (side-by-side, stacked, or PIP) via FFmpeg with proper aspect ratio. Batch save or copy raw clip files.

⌨

Keyboard Shortcuts

Full keyboard control for playback, navigation, layout switching, muting, and more. 20+ shortcuts for efficient workflow.

🛠

Smart Clip Management

Browse clips in a sortable drawer, navigate with shortcuts, and manage your dashcam footage effortlessly. Supports 100+ clips per folder.
