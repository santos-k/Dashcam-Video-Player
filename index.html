Features

Everything for Dashcam Review

From quick review to professional export — all in one lightweight desktop app

New in v2.0
📡

Wi-Fi Dashcam Connection

Connect to your dashcam over Wi-Fi. Auto-discovers the device IP, browse & download files remotely, RTSP live stream, camera controls, and device settings.

New in v2.0
🎨

Thumbnail Clip Browser

Browse clips with video thumbnails in grid or list view. Multi-select with batch delete, auto-scroll to current clip, and instant view switching.

New in v2.0
⌨

Customizable Shortcuts

30+ keyboard shortcuts, all remappable. Visual editor with conflict detection. Bindings persist across sessions. Every tooltip updates dynamically.

Core
📷

Dual Camera Sync

Auto-pairs front & back camera files by timestamp with ±5s tolerance. Supports video_front/video_back folders and F/B filename suffixes.

Core
🎬

3 Layout Modes

Side-by-side, Stacked, and Picture-in-Picture. PIP is fully draggable and resizable with proportional repositioning when controls toggle.

Core
⏱

Variable Speed (0.1x–5x)

11 speed levels from 0.1x slow-motion to 5x fast-forward. Speed persists across clip changes. Perfect for timelapse review.

🌎

GPS & Interactive Map

OpenStreetMap sidebar with device location, coordinate extraction, 3 tile layers, and links to Google Maps & OSM.

💾

FFmpeg Export

Export composed videos in any layout with H.264 encoding. Sync offset applied automatically. Real-time progress tracking.

🛠

Smart Auto-Hide UI

Controls disappear after 5s during playback. Video fills the entire screen. Move the mouse or tap to bring them back.
